# Game Assets

## Kenney Top-down Shooter (CC0)

Map, character, and UI assets are from the **Kenney Top-down Shooter** pack.

- **Source:** [Kenney – Top-down Shooter](https://www.kenney.nl/assets/top-down-shooter)
- **License:** CC0 (Public Domain). Use freely in any project, including commercial; attribution optional.
- **Location:** `public/assets/kenney-topdown-shooter/`

### Structure

- **Spritesheet/** – `spritesheet_characters.png` + `spritesheet_characters.xml` (character atlas), `spritesheet_tiles.png` (tiles).
- **Tilesheet/** – `tilesheet_complete.png`, `tilesheet_complete_2X.png` (full tilesets for map building).

Used for: player/enemy sprites (with team tint), floor/wall tiles, and future effects (muzzle, hit sparks).
